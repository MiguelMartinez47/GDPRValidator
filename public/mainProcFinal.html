GDPRValidator web-app

The main process of this application has finished, when you click on the FINISH button the program will generate the provided documents in an editable format. You can also obtain the general recomendations for cloud service providers that this tool provides by checking the checkbox on top of the finish button. REMEMBER that, although the minimum necessary documents are provided to be in a position to comply with the Data Protection Regulation, you must review them and complete them where necesary (specially in the case that you have required a DPIA template) and also perform the following actions:

  1. including information clauses in the information request forms, whether your company carries this out through paper forms or a website.

  2. Implementing the technical and organizational measures pointed out in the corresponding document.

  3. Reviewing the contracts your company currently use, update them accordingly with the contractual clauses and sign them on the last page.

  4. Detailing the contracts that your company does not have yet and also include the contractual clauses and sign them in the last page.

  5. Saving and keeping all the mentioned updated documents.

  6. Remember that you should not send these documents to your National Data Protection Agency, unless they request them.
